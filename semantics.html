Meet Elizabeth Wairimu Mbugua, a passionate individual whose interests span a diverse range of activities. From her love for swimming, she finds solace and tranquility in the water, and through volunteering, she wholeheartedly dedicates her time to making a positive impact in her community. When she's not exploring new destinations and immersing herself in different cultures during her travels, she can often be found curled up with a good book, losing herself in captivating stories. As a part-time article writer, Elizabeth is driven by her desire to expand her horizons and transition into the world of technology. Currently, she is pursuing her front-end development studies at Azubi Africa, where her enthusiasm lies in contributing to Africa's technological awakening. Elizabeth firmly believes that Africa holds boundless untapped potential, and she is determined to play her part in propelling the continent to reach the technological heights of the Western world. With a strong sense of responsibility towards her motherland, she is resolute in pulling her weight to bring about positive change and make this vision a reality.
